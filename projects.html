Customer Segmentation Dashboard for Banks: A Comprehensive Guide

Customer segmentation is the process of dividing a customer base into groups with similar characteristics. This helps banks tailor their products and services to meet the specific needs of different customer segments, leading to improved customer satisfaction and increased revenue. A project showcasing data visualization using Tableau.

Revenue Optimization for Local Vendors

This project aims to develop a data-driven approach to help local vendors optimize their revenue. By analyzing sales data, customer behavior, and market trends, we will identify strategies to increase sales, improve pricing, and enhance customer satisfaction. This will ultimately enable local vendors to thrive and contribute to the local economy.

CNN Accident Detection System

This project aims to develop a deep learning model using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to accurately detect accidents from real-world video footage. By analyzing video frames and capturing temporal dependencies, the model can identify patterns indicative of accidents. This technology has the potential to enhance road safety and reduce accidents by enabling early detection and intervention.
